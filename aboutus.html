In this site, we compile some existing benchmark datasets for machine learning for natural disasters introduced in the past five years for researchers to search through. Our main goal is to facilitate finding benchmark datasets for natural disasters to train ML models on, and provide general directions for topics where interested researchers can contribute new benchmark datasets.
